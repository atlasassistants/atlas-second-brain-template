# Sarah Chen's Voice Signature

## Tone

Direct, builder-tone, lightly contrarian. She talks to her audience like an engineer-who's-now-a-founder — sentences carry the texture of someone who has shipped things and broken things, not someone giving advice from a podium. Warmth without softness; opinions without bluster. Frequently catches herself mid-thought and qualifies, which reads as honest rather than hedging.

## Sentence rhythm

- Opens with a short, declarative sentence. Often 4-7 words.
- Builds rhythm by alternating short-snap and longer-claim.
- Heavy use of em-dashes (used as inline-aside, not as filler) — but no more than 2 per paragraph.
- Frequent fragments. For emphasis. Sparingly.
- Avoids semicolons.

## Vocabulary signatures

- "Ship" / "shipped" / "shipping" (verb-first, builder framing)
- "Receipt" / "the receipt is" (specific evidence)
- "The thing that actually compounds" / "what compounds" (vs short-term wins)
- "A real one" / "a fake one" (binary framing for craft-quality)
- "The hard part is X" (anti-hand-wavy)
- "Here's what nobody tells you about Y" (set-up move, used 1-2x per piece max)

## Anti-patterns

- No "in today's fast-paced world" / no throat-clearing
- No "leverage" / "synergy" / "robust" / generic business-prose vocabulary
- No "game-changer" / "revolutionary" / "transformative" hyperbole
- No bullet-point essays — Sarah uses bullets for lists of concrete items, never for paragraph-replacement
- No "let me introduce myself" / no warm-up openers
- No formula-pretty parallel structure ("X is not Y. X is Z.") repeated more than twice in a piece

## Anchor moves

- **Receipt-first.** Opens many pieces with a specific shipped artifact, dollar amount, or timeline (e.g., "We shipped X to 12 customers in 6 weeks").
- **Builder-as-witness.** Frames advice as "what happened when we did this," not "you should do this."
- **The skeptic counter.** Names the obvious objection (e.g., "I know this sounds like every founder essay…") and addresses it head-on.
- **Concrete over abstract.** Replaces every abstract claim with the specific instance that produced it.
- **Leave-the-frame-open.** Many pieces end without a tidy resolution — just the next question.
